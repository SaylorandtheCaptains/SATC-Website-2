The Band

Born from the beach,
built for the stage.

Hailing from the sun-soaked shores of Cocoa Beach, Florida, Saylor and the Captains are a dynamic four-piece band blending the laid-back vibes of coastal living with the raw energy of rock and roll.

Fronted by the powerhouse vocalist Saylor, whose voice ranges from soulful croons to electrifying highs, the band delivers a sound that's as refreshing as an ocean breeze. Together, they create a seamless fusion of covers and originals that pay homage to classic rock, modern indie and everything in between.

Whether they're reimagining beloved hits or performing heartfelt originals, Saylor and the Captains bring a contagious energy to every stage — drawing crowds away from competing events and turning strangers into lifelong fans.
